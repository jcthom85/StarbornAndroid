## Major Characters

### Commander Rylos (The Villain)
*   **Role:** The "Father" of the Phantom Project.
*   **Chain of command:** Answers to Orbital Compliance (Lt. Arden Vale).
*   **Location:** The Core (Boss Arena).
*   **Appearance:** Cybernetically enhanced soldier. Half his face is chrome. He speaks without moving his mouth.
*   **Personality:** Possessive. He doesn't hate Gh0st; he is *disappointed* in him.
*   **Key Line:** "You are malfunctioning, Unit 734. Submit for correction. Pain is just data leaving the body."
*   **Fate:** Defeated by the party. Executed by Vale via remote kill-switch (demonstrating Vale's ruthlessness).

### Lt. Arden Vale (The Voice)
*   **Role:** The New Boss.
*   **Location:** Hologram / Comms.
*   **Function:** Stinger Reveal.
*   **Key Line:** "Director Thorne's metrics are... inefficient. We are initiating a reboot. Goodbye, Commander."

## Bestiary (World 4)

### Magma Drone (Machine - Common)
*   **Location:** Slag Pits.
*   **Behavior:** Flying. Sprays Napalm (Burn DoT).
*   **Weakness:** Freeze.

### Slag Golem (Elemental - Common)
*   **Location:** Slag Pits.
*   **Behavior:** Heavy melee. Splits into smaller slimes on death.
*   **Weakness:** Blast Wave (Shatter).

### Welder Bot (Machine - Common)
*   **Location:** Assembly Line.
*   **Behavior:** Melee. Uses torch for high burn damage. Explodes on death.
*   **Weakness:** Ranged (Keep away).

### Flame Trooper (Human - Elite)
*   **Location:** Assembly Line.
*   **Behavior:** Uses Flamethrower (Cone AoE). Heavily armored.
*   **Weakness:** Pierce / Source Art: Stasis.

### Phantom Prototype (Elite Machine)
*   **Location:** Assembly Line.
*   **Behavior:** Fast, stealthy, uses energy blades. A "proto-Gh0st".
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: True Sight / Shock.

### Conveyor Crusher (Hazard/Enemy)
*   **Location:** Assembly Line.
*   **Behavior:** Stationary industrial press. Slams down on a rhythm. Invulnerable.
*   **Weakness:** Timing / Hacking the console.

### Titan Walker (Boss Vehicle)
*   **Pilot:** Commander Rylos.
*   **Phase 1:** Ranged Missiles.
*   **Phase 2:** Melee Stomp (Platform sinking mechanic).
*   **Weakness:** Hitting the cooling vents exposed after attacks.
