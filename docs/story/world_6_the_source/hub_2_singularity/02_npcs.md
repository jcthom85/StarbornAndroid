## Final Antagonist

### The Ascended (Final Boss)
*   **Role:** The Soloist of the Great Silence.
*   **Phase 1:** Humanoid (Vale). Focuses on "Silence" debuffs and "Compliance" beams.
*   **Phase 2:** God-Form (The Singularity). Reality warping attacks. Summons shadow clones of the party.
*   **Phase 3:** The Core. A DPS race to "Tune the World" before the Silence wipe.

## Bestiary (World 6)

### Source Shadow (Digital - Common)
*   **Appearance:** A flickering, low-res shadow of World 1 enemies.
*   **Behavior:** Unstable movement. Explodes into "Noise" on death.
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: Blast Wave.

### Distorted Sentinel (Machine - Common)
*   **Appearance:** A World 3 Sentinel but twisted into impossible geometry.
*   **Behavior:** Fires "Glitch Bolts" that scramble character turn orders.
*   **Weakness:** Hacking / Shock.

### Glitch Hound (Beast - Common)
*   **Appearance:** A Shard-Hound from World 2 but flickering in and out of existence.
*   **Behavior:** High Evasion. Teleports behind characters for "Backstab" damage.
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: Scan (True Sight).

### Memory Leak (Digital - Elite)
*   **Appearance:** A massive, pulsing data-blob that leaks images of Jed, Thorne, and Elara.
*   **Behavior:** Drains "Erosion" (Narrative cost) / Applies massive debuffs.
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: Link / Focus fire.

### Nightmare Guard (Humanoid - Elite)
*   **Appearance:** A Dominion Guard wearing the faces of the people Nova couldn't save.
*   **Behavior:** Uses "Guilt" (Silence effect). Heavy shielding.
*   **Weakness:** Melee (Gh0st).
