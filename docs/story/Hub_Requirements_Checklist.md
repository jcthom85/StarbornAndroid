# Hub Requirements Checklist

This document tracks the **Entry State** (prerequisites) and **Exit State** (deliverables) for every Hub in the game. Use this to ensure narrative continuity and system progression when designing detailed content.

---

## World 1: The Mines
**Theme:** Origins / Industrial Horror

### Hub 1.1: Homestead Quarter
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova (Solo).
    *   **Items:** Old Mining Pistol, zero credits.
    *   **Goal:** Survive another shift, maybe scavenge something good.
    *   **Mood:** Routine, oppressive but safe-ish.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Tutorials:** Movement, Investigation (Tap words), Inventory (Equip Pistol).
    *   **Key Location:** Nova’s Bunk (Save Point tutorial).
    *   **Key NPC:** Jed (Father figure, gives first real quest).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova (Solo).
    *   **Items:** Mining Access Pass (Key Item).
    *   **Story Beat:** Nova heads to the mines for a "special job" from Jed.
    *   **Next:** Transit Checkpoint → Hub 1.2.

### Hub 1.2: Logistics Sector
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova (Solo).
    *   **Goal:** Get through the checkpoint and descend to the deep mines.
    *   **Mood:** Bureaucratic, sterile, high security.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Key NPC:** Zeke (introduced via screens/voice first, then in person).
    *   **Key Location:** The Deep Elevator (Point of No Return).
    *   **Event:** The Relic Chamber (Nova finds **Tuning Fork**).
    *   **Combat:** First real fight (Drones). **Tuning Fork** grants **Blast** (Source Art).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova + Zeke (Defector).
*   **Items:** Tuning Fork (Relic 1), Chime (Key Item/Resonator).
    *   **Status:** Fugitives. Colony on lockdown. Jed is dead.
    *   **Next:** The Crash Interlude (Pod Escape).

---

## Interlude: The Crash
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova + Zeke.
    *   **Vehicle:** Stolen Cargo Pod.
    *   **Goal:** Escape the planetary shield.
*   **Content Requirements:**
*   **Action:** Zeke splices the Chime into the pod for a burst of Source thrust.
    *   **Beat:** Nav locks to Orion’s signal. Shield hits them.
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Status:** Crashed. Pod destroyed.
    *   **Next:** World 2 Start.

---

## World 2: The Wilds
**Theme:** Survival / Ancient History

### Hub 2.1: Jungle Ruins (Sector 9)
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Nova + Zeke (Injured/Shaken).
    *   **Goal:** Survive. Find the signal source.
    *   **Mood:** Alien, bioluminescent, dangerous.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Recruit:** Orion (Awakened from Stasis).
    *   **Recruit:** Gh0st (Hunter turned Protector via Elara Protocol).
    *   **Relic:** **The Bridge** (Grants **Link**).
    *   **Ship:** *The Astra* is found here (Derelict state).
    *   **Breathers:** Canopy Ridge (Vista), Tideglass Beach (Rest).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Party:** Full Crew (Nova, Zeke, Orion, Gh0st).
    *   **Vehicle:** *The Astra* (Repaired, Flight Capable).
    *   **Items:** Bridge Relic.
    *   **Next:** Fly to World 3 (The Spire).

---

## World 3: The Spire
**Theme:** Heist / Class Warfare

### Hub 3.1: Lower City
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Full Crew.
    *   **Goal:** Find a way to the Upper City to steal the Lens.
    *   **Mood:** Cyberpunk, rain, neon, poverty.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Stealth/Disguise:** Blending in.
    *   **Key NPC:** Local contact/criminal element (optional).
    *   **Breather:** Night Market (Culture/Food).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Access:** Elevator Codes / Smuggler Route secured.
    *   **Next:** Elevator Ride → Hub 3.2.

### Hub 3.2: Upper City
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Full Crew.
    *   **Goal:** Infiltrate the Administrator’s Penthouse.
    *   **Mood:** Clean, white gold, fake utopia.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **The Heist:** Multi-stage infiltration.
    *   **Boss:** The Administrator.
    *   **Relic:** **The Lens** (Grants **Scan**).
    *   **Breather:** Skypark (Fake Nature).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Items:** The Lens Relic.
    *   **Intel:** Planetary Shield has a frequency gap, but *Astra* needs better engines to reach the Ring.
    *   **Next:** World 4 (The Foundry).

---

## World 4: The Foundry
**Theme:** Industry / Vengeance

### Hub 4.1: Slag Pits (Sector 7)
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Full Crew.
    *   **Goal:** Steal a Deep-Core Engine.
    *   **Mood:** Hellscape, heat, metal, noise.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Character Arc:** Gh0st confronts his origin/creator.
    *   **Relic:** **The Anvil** (Grants **Construct**).
    *   **Boss:** Commander Rylos (Gh0st’s rival).
    *   **Breather:** Cooling Springs.
    *   **Stinger:** Vale executes Thorne (Off-screen or on-screen via feed).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Ship:** *The Astra* upgraded with Deep-Core Engine AND **Phase-Cutter Weapons**.
    *   **Items:** Anvil Relic.
    *   **Next:** Launch to World 5 (The Void).

---

## World 5: The Void
**Theme:** Cosmic Horror / Revelation

### Hub 5.1: Orbital Ring
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Full Crew.
    *   **Goal:** Stop "Ascension." Rescue Elara.
    *   **Mood:** Sterile station, zero-g elements, uncanny silence.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Breather:** Solarium Deck (The final fake paradise).
    *   **Relic:** **The Anchor** (Grants **Stasis**).
    *   **Boss:** Vale (Human Form).
    *   **Event:** Find **Deposed Director Thorne** hiding in the Server Farm.
    *   **Event:** The Tear opens. Elara is lost. Vale Ascends.
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Party:** Pulled into the Tear.
    *   **Items:** Anchor Relic.
    *   **Next:** World 6 (The Source).

---

## World 6: The Source
**Theme:** Surreal / Finale

### Hub 6.1: Event Horizon
*   **Entry State:**
    *   **Party:** Separated (initially).
    *   **Goal:** Reunite and Tune the World.
    *   **Mood:** Dream logic, memory fragments, glitchy geometry.
*   **Content Requirements:**
    *   **Gauntlet:** Dealing with each character’s nightmare loop.
    *   **Final Boss:** The Ascended (Vale God-Form).
    *   **Relic:** **The Key** (Completed Set).
*   **Exit State:**
    *   **Ending:** The world is tuned. Nova loses powers. Credits.
