# Locations — World 2: The Wilds (Hub 4: Sector 9 Ruins)

## Hub Layout
The ancient Aethel facility buried deep beneath the jungle. Constructed of smooth, dark stone panels, the interior is clean of organic overgrowth, lit only by soft, pulsing light murals and standing liquid cymatic geometry. It serves as the resting place of *The Astra* and Orion's stasis vault.

---

## 1. Hall of Echoes (`hall_of_echoes`)
*   **Icon:** 🏛️
*   **Description:** The massive entryway hall and archival wings of the facility, showing history through light patterns.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_foyer_grand_hall` (Entry Hall): The main entrance hall with high vaulted ceilings.
    *   `sector9_foyer_left_gallery` (Architect Reliquary): Display cases containing cracked resonance crystals.
    *   `sector9_foyer_right_gallery` (Aethel Archives): Racks of wave logging cylinders storing the memories of the Aethel.
    *   `sector9_foyer_security_hub` (Dominion Outpost): An abandoned corporate desk with a hackable command terminal.
    *   `sector9_foyer_vault_door` (Hangar Deck Portal): A heavy stone vault door leading directly to the hangar (locked).
    *   `sector9_conduit_crawlspace` (Ventilation Conduit): A narrow ventilation duct bypassing the vault door.
    *   `sector9_conduit_junction` (T-Junction Wiring): A wiring junction box where security systems can be rerouted.
    *   `sector9_conduit_breaker_room` (Auxiliary Generators): Generator panels supplying local emergency light cells.
    *   `sector9_conduit_access_shaft` (Access Ladder): A vertical ladder leading up to the high stasis vents.
    *   `sector9_hall_of_echoes` (Mural Corridor): The grand hallway lined with standing light murals showing the history of Harmony.
    *   `sector9_hall_echo_alcoves` (Visual Records): Small alcoves displaying standing wave patterns.
    *   `sector9_hall_acoustics_lab` (Research Station): Dominion scientific rigs analyzing Aethel frequencies.
    *   `sector9_hall_reflection_pool` (Cymatic Basin): A shallow stone basin containing water shaped into geometric standing waves.
    *   `sector9_archive_vault` (Cylinder Vault): Rows of storage cylinders holding ancient Aethel data.
    *   `sector9_archive_reading_room` (Interface Console): A stone terminal where the cylinder logs can be played.
    *   `sector9_archive_tuning_bay` (Matrix Puzzle): A column puzzle requiring the alignment of three frequencies to a C#m7 chord.
    *   `sector9_archive_secret_stash` (Hidden Relic Room): The vault containing the **Link Relic** upgrade.

## 2. Stasis Chamber (`stasis_chamber`)
*   **Icon:** ❄️
*   **Description:** The cryogenic vault containing Orion's pod and the high ventilation network.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_stasis_chamber` (Orion's Podium): The circular room containing Orion's stasis pod.
    *   `sector9_stasis_ring_array` (Alignment Rings): The alignment console controlling the energy rings around the pod.
    *   `sector9_stasis_coolant_lines` (Erosion Buffers): Blue cooling lines absorbing the heat of the pod containment.
    *   `sector9_stasis_observation` (Observation Deck): A glass-walled balcony looking down at the stasis podium.
    *   `sector9_vents_gantry` (High Gantry): A metallic catwalk hanging high above the pod deck.
    *   `sector9_vents_access_grate` (Infiltration Hatch): The vent grate allowing access to the maintenance shafts.
    *   `sector9_vents_tech_alcove` (Transmitter Site): An alcove containing a forgotten Dominion signal transmitter (quest item).

## 3. Source Gate (`source_gate`)
*   **Icon:** 🎼
*   **Description:** The power distribution dynamo and the acoustic barrier blocking access to the Hangar.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_power_turbine` (Source Dynamo): A massive glowing turbine dynamo humming at a deafening pitch.
    *   `sector9_power_manifold` (Fluid Distribution): Heavy pipes carrying liquid energy fuel to the dynamo.
    *   `sector9_power_breaker_deck` (Main Switch): The terminal where the hangar barrier breaker switches are located.
    *   `sector9_power_valve` (Emergency Release): A manual valve to dump excess charge during overload.
    *   `sector9_source_gate` (Acoustic Barrier Emitter): The gateway blocked by a solid wall of acoustic force.
    *   `sector9_gate_emitter_left` (Harmonic Horn): The generator projecting the left frequency wave.
    *   `sector9_gate_emitter_right` (Resonance Cup): The grounding cup absorbing the right frequency wave.

## 4. Astra Hangar Bay (`hangar_bay`)
*   **Icon:** 🛸
*   **Description:** The cavernous launch bay containing *The Astra*.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_hangar_bay` (Astra Pad): The launch pad where the damaged hull of *The Astra* is docked.
    *   `sector9_hangar_scaffolding` (Maintenance Gantry): Walkways wrapping around the ship's engines and hull.
    *   `sector9_hangar_staging` (Cargo Staging): A platform loading bay filled with old crates.
    *   `sector9_hangar_tower` (Control Console): The primary flight deck terminal controlling launch operations.
    *   `sector9_hangar_fuel_lines` (Conduit Hookups): Fuel lines connecting the ship to the facility's reserves.
    *   `sector9_hangar_lock` (Ceiling Blast Shields): The massive ceiling shutters that slide open to reveal the sky.

## 5. The Sky (`the_sky`)
*   **Icon:** ☁️
*   **Description:** The high mesosphere climb where the ship encounters the Planetary Shield.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_the_sky` (Climbing Flight): *The Astra* climbing out of the Sector 9 atmosphere.
    *   `sector9_sky_mesosphere` (Storm Cells): Heavy lightning clouds threatening the flight controls.
    *   `sector9_sky_shield_threshold` (Shield Edge): The physical boundary directly below the Planetary Shield.
    *   `sector9_sky_sensor_buoy` (Dominion Sat): A floating sensor monitoring atmospheric anomalies.
    *   `sector9_sky_barrier_core` (Frequency Node): The central node generating the local phase field.
    *   `sector9_sky_hard_deck` (The Boundary): The point of impact where the Shield discharge pushes the ship back.
