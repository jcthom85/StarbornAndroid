## Enemies (The Wilds & The Ruins)

### Shard-Hound (Wildlife)
*   **Type:** Beast / Fast.
*   **Behavior:** High speed and evasion. Attacks with crystalline claws. Often hunts in packs.
*   **Weakness:** Stun / Stasis.

### Spore-Spitter (Wildlife)
*   **Type:** Beast / Ranged.
*   **Behavior:** Stationary but fires toxic spores that apply "Poison" (DoT).
*   **Weakness:** Burn / Physical.

### Ruin-Guardian (Machine)
*   **Type:** Tech / Melee.
*   **Behavior:** Ancient Aethel security droid. Slow, heavy strikes. Uses a "Source Shield" that must be broken.
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: Blast Wave.

### Razor-Vine (Hazard)
*   **Type:** Beast / Trap.
*   **Behavior:** Camouflaged against jungle walls. Lashes out at passing targets to Root them.
*   **Weakness:** Burn / Scythes.

### Stalker-Vine (Elite Wildlife)
*   **Type:** Elite / Support.
*   **Behavior:** Larger, sentient vine that can pull allies out of position and heal other beasts.
*   **Weakness:** High Burst Damage.

### Sentinel Orb (Elite Machine)
*   **Type:** Elite / Tech.
*   **Behavior:** Floating Aethel drone. Fires "Phase Beams" that ignore armor. Can teleport locally.
*   **Weakness:** Source Art: Scan / Link.

### The Beast (Boss)
*   **Type:** Source Beast.
*   **Role:** Apex Predator.
*   **Appearance:** A silverback gorilla mutated by raw Source leakage. Glowing purple veins, bark-like armor.
*   **Behavior:** Territorially aggressive. It wants the Chime.
