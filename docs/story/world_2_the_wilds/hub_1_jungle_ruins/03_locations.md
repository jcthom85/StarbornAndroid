# Locations — World 2: The Wilds (Hub 3: Jungle Ruins)

## Hub Layout
The surface of Sector 9 is a dense, bioluminescent jungle climbing over smooth, resonant Architect stone. Key sub-zones progress from the escape pod crash site, through choice paths (the spore-choked Thickets vs the Glow-Water Beach delta), ascending to the Canopy Ridge, and concluding at the Temple Gate.

---

## 1. Crash Site Sector (`sector9_landing`)
*   **Icon:** 🔥
*   **Description:** The smoking wreckage of the escape pod embedded in mud and giant jungle roots.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_crash_site` (Crash Site Core): The escape pod wreckage, half-submerged in swamp. Zeke lies injured on a medical stretcher here.
    *   `sector9_pod_interior` (Escape Pod Cockpit): The cramped, smoking interior of the pod. Contains flight log tapes and a survival inventory locker.
    *   `sector9_landing_brush` (Scorched Brush): Fire-scarred jungle trees and scattered batteries from the pod launch cells.
    *   `sector9_landing_glade` (Overgrown Glade): A small clearing of giant ferns and bioluminescent mushrooms.
    *   `sector9_landing_stream` (Stream Entrance): A muddy bank where glowing blue water leads eastward.
    *   `sector9_landing_perimeter` (Security Perimeter): An outer boundary marked by a smashed Dominion sensor drone.
    *   `sector9_landing_drop` (Pre-crash Pod Fragment): A piece of metal casing that sheared off during descent. Yields components for tinkering.

---

## 2. Razor-Vine Path (`razor_vine_path`)
*   **Icon:** 🌿
*   **Description:** A treacherous path overgrown with toxic flora, leading directly up to the Canopy.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_canopy` (Thorn Gate): A bottleneck blocked by thick, writhing Razor-Vines that react to movement.
    *   `sector9_wilds_thickets` (Spore Thickets): A foggy path choked with Spore-Spitters.
    *   `sector9_wilds_nest` (Hound Nesting Grounds): Jagged rocks and hollow roots where Shard-Hounds sleep.
    *   `sector9_wilds_hollow` (Poison Hollow): A depression where toxic spores pool, draining Nova's Guard state.
    *   `sector9_wilds_grave` (Patrol Graveyard): The remains of a Dominion scouting party. Nova retrieves the **Thermal Cutter** blueprint from a datapad here.
    *   `sector9_wilds_overlook` (Canopy Edge): A high cliff overlooking the green expanse below.
    *   `sector9_wilds_canopy_walk` (Hanging Canopy Bridge): A natural suspension bridge formed by massive tree roots.
    *   `sector9_wilds_archway` (Ancient Gateway): A tuned stone archway filtering the wind into warm minor chords.
    *   `sector9_wilds_dense_brush` (Beast Run): Flattened brush and heavy footsteps warning of apex wildlife.
    *   `sector9_wilds_clearance` (Burned Glade): A clearing where a wild fire has left scorched ash.
    *   `sector9_wilds_lookout` (Hunter's Blind): Gh0st's temporary lookout post, littered with sniper shell casings.

---

## 3. Tideglass Beach (`tideglass_beach`)
*   **Icon:** 🌊
*   **Description:** A hidden cove where the jungle meets the glowing sea. Offers a peaceful bypass to the thickets.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_stream_pools` (Mist Pools): Bioluminescent pools where mist hangs low.
    *   `sector9_stream_wetlands` (Resonant Sedge): Whispering reeds that hum in harmony with the current.
    *   `sector9_stream_flats` (Crystalline Flats): A beach of glittering, fine silver sand.
    *   `sector9_stream_falls` (Resonant Falls): A cascading waterfall dropping over tuned stone tiers.
    *   `sector9_stream_tidepools` (Beach Transition): The delta where the stream widens into the glowing ocean tide.
    *   `sector9_stream_cave_entrance` (Cavern Entrance): A dark opening behind the falls.
    *   `sector9_stream_cave_depths` (Glow-Moss Cavern): Wet stone tunnels lit by bioluminescent moss.
    *   `sector9_stream_sunken_passage` (Waterlogged Tunnel): A fully submerged bypass requiring focus to swim through.
    *   `sector9_beach_cliffs` (High Overlook): A steep stone shelf looking down at the coastline.
    *   `sector9_beach_cove` (Sandy Cove): A peaceful breather campsite containing a cooking campfire.
    *   `sector9_beach_pools` (Tide Pools): Glow-Water tide pools containing tideglass skewers (fishable).
    *   `sector9_beach_cargo` (Beached Cargo Wreck): A crashed Dominion cargo container containing raw metal scrap.
    *   `sector9_beach_cave` (Sea Cavern): Caves carved by tides, echoing with blowhole sound effects.
    *   `sector9_beach_pillar` (Sunken Obelisk): A half-submerged Architect obelisk that hums at low tide.
    *   `sector9_beach_grotto` (Hidden Grotto): A glowing cave. Contains the **Resin Mod** for weapons.
    *   `sector9_beach_dunes` (Star-Sand Dunes): Singing dunes that emit low resonant hums when walked on.

---

## 4. Canopy Ridge (`canopy_ridge`)
*   **Icon:** ⛰️
*   **Description:** A wind-carved stone plateau high above the jungle canopy, directly under the Planetary Shield.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_ridge_climb` (Vine Climb): A vertical cliff face climbed using thick jungle vines.
    *   `sector9_ridge_ledges` (Windy Ledges): High stone shelves exposed to freezing winds.
    *   `sector9_ridge_overhang` (Beast Lair Approach): A rocky overhang littered with bone fragments.
    *   `sector9_canopy_ridge` (Canopy Ridge Arena): The wide stone shelf where the mutated **Source Beast** boss is fought.
    *   `sector9_ridge_viewpoint` (Shield Viewpoint): An outlook where the party rests and looks at the Planetary Shield ceiling.
    *   `sector9_ridge_sniper_perch` (Sniper Lookout): The elevated position where Gh0st ambushes the party.
    *   `sector9_ridge_crevice` (High Pass): A narrow fissure in the rock leading to the temple gateway.
    *   `sector9_ridge_plateau` (Sanctuary Outlook): A flat plateau directly facing the Temple.

---

## 5. Temple Gate (`temple_gate`)
*   **Icon:** ⛩️
*   **Description:** The massive, smooth stone courtyard leading into the ancient facility.
*   **Rooms:**
    *   `sector9_temple_plaza` (Stone Courtyard): Perfectly flat stone courtyard, free of jungle vegetation.
    *   `sector9_temple_vestibule` (Outer Guard Post): Deactivated security turrets covered in vines.
    *   `sector9_temple_lock_chamber` (Resonator Lock): The central gate door containing a slot for the Chime.
    *   `sector9_temple_left_wing` (Resonator Column): Left tuning column, puzzle interactable.
    *   `sector9_temple_right_wing` (Frequency Dampener): Right tuning column, puzzle interactable.
    *   `sector9_temple_gate` (Inner Gate Threshold): The dark hallway directly leading inside the facility.
